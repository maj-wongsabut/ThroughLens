Help and Support

Need help navigating Through Lens? You're in the right place!


Frequently Asked Questions:

  1. How do I upload photos?
    Log in to your account and click on the "Upload" button in the top menu. Follow the steps to add your images.
  2. How do I edit or delete my photos?
    Visit your profile, select the photo you want to manage, and choose either "Edit" or "Delete" from the options.
  3. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
    Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

Still need help? If you can't find the answer to your question, contact us at support@throughlens.com, and we'll assist you as soon as possible.
